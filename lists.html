Ingredients

  • 1kg potatoes
  • 100ml milk
  • 50g salted butter
  • Freshly grated nutmeg
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Mashed Potatoes

  1. Chop potatos into quarters
  2. Simmer in salted water for 15-20 minutes util tender
  3. Hheat milk, butter, and nutmeg
  4. Drain potatoes and mash
  5. Mix in the milk mixture
